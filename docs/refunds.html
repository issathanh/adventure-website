Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

Overview

My Adventure uses a credit-based system for story generation. This policy explains when refunds are available and how to request them.

Important: All purchases are made through the Apple App Store and are subject to Apple's refund policies. We do not process payments directly.

Apple App Store Refunds

If you want to request a refund for a credit purchase:

  1. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your My Adventure purchase
  4. Click "Report a Problem"
  5. Follow Apple's refund request process

Apple will review your request according to their policies. Refund decisions are made by Apple, not YourAdventure.

Credit Refunds (Technical Issues)

Automatic Refunds

Credits are automatically refunded in the following situations:

  • Server error prevents story generation
  • AI service timeout or failure
  • Database connection issues
  • App crashes during scene generation
  • Any technical failure on our end

These refunds are processed immediately and require no action from you.

Manual Credit Refund Requests

If you experienced a technical issue but credits were not automatically refunded:

  1. Email youradventuresp@gmail.com
  2. Include your account email
  3. Describe the issue
  4. Note the approximate date/time

We will investigate and process legitimate refunds within 2-3 business days.

When Credits Are NOT Refunded

Content Policy Violations

Credits are NOT refunded when prompts are rejected for violating our content policy, including:

  • Violence or disturbing content
  • Inappropriate or adult themes
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Explicit or sexual content

Why? Content filtering uses AI services that incur costs, and charging for violations deters inappropriate use.

v2.1.0 Update: Our content policy system has been optimized to reduce false positives while maintaining family-friendly standards. If you believe your prompt was incorrectly rejected, contact support for review.

User Error or Dissatisfaction

Credits are not refunded for:

  • Not liking the story output
  • Typos in your prompt
  • Changing your mind after generation
  • Accidental taps or selections
  • Stories you don't want to continue

Successful Generation

If story generation completes successfully (even if you dislike the result), credits are not refunded. The AI performed the requested service.

Refund Process Timeline

  • Technical Issues: Automatic refunds are instant. Manual requests processed within 2-3 business days.
  • Apple Refunds: Typically processed within 48 hours, but Apple's timeline may vary.
  • Credit Balance: Refunded credits appear in your account immediately after processing.

No Refunds After Account Deletion

Once you request account deletion, all unused credits are forfeited. We cannot refund credits after account deletion is complete.

Fair Use Policy

We reserve the right to:

  • Deny refund requests that appear fraudulent
  • Suspend accounts that abuse the refund system
  • Investigate patterns of repeated refund requests

Credit Expiration

Credits never expire. You don't need to request a refund because of inactivity - your credits will be waiting whenever you're ready to use them.

Partial Refunds

We do not offer partial refunds on credit packs. If you request a refund through Apple:

  • Apple may approve a full refund of the purchase
  • All credits from that purchase will be removed from your account
  • Any credits already used will be deducted from future purchases if necessary

Disputed Charges

If you see a charge you don't recognize:

  1. Check your Apple purchase history
  2. Verify it wasn't a family member's purchase (Family Sharing)
  3. Contact Apple Support if the charge is unauthorized
  4. Email us at youradventuresp@gmail.com for assistance

Refund Exceptions

In rare cases, we may offer credit refunds or compensation for:

  • Extended service outages (24+ hours)
  • Major bugs affecting all users
  • Policy changes that significantly impact user experience

These decisions are made on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed.

Consumer Rights

This policy does not affect your statutory consumer rights. In some jurisdictions, you may have additional rights beyond those described here.

If you believe your consumer rights have been violated, you may:

  • Contact your local consumer protection agency
  • Escalate through Apple's dispute resolution
  • Seek legal advice

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
