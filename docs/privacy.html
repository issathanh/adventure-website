Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to My Adventure ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using My Adventure, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and profile information provided through our secure authentication service
  • Story Content: Character descriptions, story choices, and generated stories you create
  • Transaction Information: Purchase history, credit balance, and payment information (processed securely by Apple)

Social Media Information

  • Profile Information: Username, bio, location, website, profile banner
  • Social Interactions: Stories you like, comments you post, users you follow
  • Public Stories: Stories you choose to share publicly with descriptions and tags
  • Forked Stories: Stories you've remixed from other users
  • Engagement Data: Follower/following counts, like counts, comment counts

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and app version
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, stories created, time spent in app, and interaction patterns
  • Error Logs: Crash reports and error logs to improve app stability (via error monitoring services)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the YourAdventure service
  • Generate AI-powered stories based on your prompts
  • Process purchases and manage your credit balance
  • Send notifications about your account and app updates
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Enable social features including follows, likes, comments, and story forking
  • Show your public stories in community feeds and search results
  • Send notifications about social interactions (likes, comments, new followers)
  • Display user profiles and social statistics
  • Recommend stories and creators based on your interests
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and policy violations
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

We use various third-party services to provide and improve our app functionality. These services may collect and process your data according to their own privacy policies:

Authentication Services

Secure user account management and authentication. Your email and account details are processed by our authentication provider.

Third-Party AI Services

AI story generation and content moderation. Your story prompts and generated content may be processed by AI service providers to generate stories and ensure content appropriateness.

Cloud Storage Services

Image storage and content delivery. Generated images and media content are stored and delivered through cloud infrastructure providers.

Payment Processing

In-app purchase processing. Payment information is handled by the Apple App Store. Apple Privacy Policy

Analytics and Monitoring

Error tracking, performance monitoring, and app analytics to improve stability and user experience.

Database Hosting

Secure cloud database hosting for storing your account information, stories, and app data.

Data Processing: These third-party services may process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We select service providers that maintain appropriate data protection standards, but we are not responsible for their data practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services.

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS
  • Secure Servers: Data is stored on secure servers provided by Neon (PostgreSQL)
  • Access Controls: Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular Security Audits: We regularly review and update our security practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Public Information

When you share a story publicly or create a user profile, the following information becomes visible to all YourAdventure users:

  • Your username and profile information (bio, location, website)
  • Public stories you've shared (title, description, tags, content)
  • Your follower and following counts
  • Comments you post on public stories
  • Stories you've liked (visible to story authors)

Privacy Control: You can delete public stories, comments, or make your profile private at any time from your account settings.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Stories and scenes are retained until you delete them
  • Public stories and comments remain visible until you delete them
  • Social interactions (likes, follows) are deleted when you unfollow or unlike
  • Notification history is kept for 90 days
  • Transaction records are kept for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely

When you delete your account, we permanently delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

You can request a copy of your personal data at any time.

Right to Delete

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data.

Right to Correct

You can update or correct inaccurate information in your account settings.

Right to Data Portability

You can request an export of your data in a portable format.

Right to Opt-Out

You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: youradventuresp@gmail.com

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years (legal requirement for financial records)
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 90 days for security and analytical purposes
  • Deleted Account Data: Permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request

10. Children's Privacy

YourAdventure is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at youradventuresp@gmail.com, and we will delete such information promptly.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using YourAdventure, you consent to such transfers.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Rights related to automated decision-making

Your continued use of YourAdventure after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
