Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I create my first story? +

1. Open the YourAdventure app

2. Tap "Create New Story"

3. Describe your character (e.g., "A brave knight searching for a dragon")

4. Wait a few seconds for the AI to generate your story opening

5. Make choices to continue your adventure!

What makes a good character description? +

Great character descriptions include:

  • Who they are (a young wizard, a curious cat, etc.)
  • What they're doing or looking for
  • Keep it family-friendly and age-appropriate

Examples:

  • "A friendly robot learning about human emotions"
  • "A magical unicorn protecting an enchanted forest"
  • "A curious mouse on a quest for the world's best cheese"

Credits & Purchases

How do credits work? +

My Adventure uses a simple credit system:

  • 1 credit = 1 story scene generation
  • 1 credit = forking (remixing) a public story
  • Each scene includes narration, artwork, and choices
  • Credits never expire
  • Available in packs of 100 and 1000 credits
  • FREE actions: viewing stories, liking, commenting, following
How do I purchase credits? (For App Store Reviewers) +

To access in-app purchases:

1. Open the app and sign in

2. Look at the TOP-LEFT corner of the screen

3. TAP the credit balance pill/counter

4. A modal will open showing available credit packs:

  • 100 Credit Pack (com.yourstory.app.v2.credit_100)
  • 1000 Credit Pack (com.yourstory.app.v2.credit_1000)

6. Credits appear in your account immediately

Alternative: The purchase modal also appears automatically when you try to fork a story without enough credits.

How do I restore my purchases? +

If you've purchased credits but they're not showing:

1. Go to Settings (gear icon)

2. Tap "Restore Purchases"

3. Your credits will be restored within a few seconds

Note: Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase.

Can I get a refund? +

Refunds are handled through Apple. See our Refund Policy for details.

Technical Issues: If you experienced a technical error that prevented story generation, we may refund the affected credits. Contact youradventuresp@gmail.com with details.

Content Policy Violations: Credits used for inappropriate prompts are non-refundable.

Do credits expire? +

No! Your credits never expire. Use them whenever you want to create stories.

Using the App

How do story choices work? +

After each scene, you'll see 2-3 choices. Selecting a choice costs 1 credit and generates the next scene based on your selection. Each story can go in many different directions!

Can I save my stories? +

Yes! All your stories are automatically saved. Access them from the "My Stories" tab at any time.

Can I turn off image generation? +

Yes! When creating or continuing a story, toggle the "Generate Images" option. This doesn't affect the credit cost - it's still 1 credit per scene.

Why was my prompt rejected? +

My Adventure uses automated content filtering powered by AI to ensure stories are family-friendly. Prompts containing violence, inappropriate content, or adult themes will be rejected.

Note: When a prompt is rejected, 1 credit is still deducted to cover the content checking cost and discourage inappropriate use.

AI Limitation: Our filtering system uses AI and may occasionally reject appropriate content or miss subtle issues. This is a limitation of automated systems. Use your discretion and try rephrasing if needed.

How long does it take to generate a scene? +

Most scenes generate in 5-15 seconds. Image generation may take an additional few seconds. Generation time depends on server load and your internet connection.

Social Features

How do I make my story public? +

From your story details screen, tap "Share Publicly" and add a description and tags. Your story will appear in community feeds and search results. You can make it private again at any time.

What is story forking? +

Forking lets you create your own version of someone else's public story. The original creator is credited, and you can take the story in a new direction. Think of it like remixing!

Can I delete my comments or public stories? +

Yes! You can delete your comments and public stories at any time from your profile or the story screen. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

How do I report inappropriate content? +

Tap the "..." menu on any public story or comment and select "Report." Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours. Users who violate our community guidelines may have their accounts suspended.

Who can see my profile information? +

Your profile (username, bio, follower counts) is visible to all My Adventure users. Your private stories are never visible to anyone but you. You can make your profile private in Settings.

Purchase Troubleshooting

I don't see any credit packs available +

If credit packs aren't showing up:

  1. Make sure you're on a real iOS device (not simulator)
  2. Products may take 4-6 hours to propagate in Apple's sandbox environment
  3. Check that your Apple ID is valid for purchases
  4. Try tapping the credit balance in the top-left corner again
  5. Restart the app and try again

If the issue persists, contact youradventuresp@gmail.com

Purchase completed but credits didn't appear +

If your purchase completed but credits aren't showing:

  1. Pull down to refresh the credit balance
  2. Force close and reopen the app
  3. Tap "Restore Purchases" in settings
  4. Wait a few minutes - processing can take time
  5. Check your Apple receipt to confirm purchase went through

If credits still don't appear after 15 minutes, contact youradventuresp@gmail.com with your Apple receipt

Can't find the purchase button +

The purchase button is NOT in settings!

To purchase credits:

  1. Look at the TOP-LEFT corner of the screen
  2. You'll see your credit balance (e.g., "50 credits")
  3. TAP the credit balance
  4. A modal will open with purchase options
  5. Tap "Add credits" to see credit packs

AI & Language Features

What languages does YourAdventure support? +

My Adventure can generate stories in multiple languages! Simply write your character description in your preferred language, and the AI will generate the story in that language.

Note: Story quality may vary by language. English typically produces the most consistent results, but we support many languages including Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, and more.

How accurate are AI-generated stories? +

Important: All stories are created by artificial intelligence and have limitations:

  • Not human-written: Stories are generated by AI, not written by professional authors
  • May contain errors: AI can make factual mistakes, grammatical errors, or logical inconsistencies
  • Quality varies: Some stories will be better than others
  • Unexpected content possible: Despite filtering, AI may occasionally generate surprising or inappropriate content
  • Use discretion: We recommend parents review content and supervise their children's use

We continuously improve our AI and filtering systems, but no automated system is perfect. Report any issues to youradventuresp@gmail.com.

Why was inappropriate content generated? +

We sincerely apologize if you encountered inappropriate content. We use multiple layers of AI-powered content filtering, but no automated system can catch 100% of issues.

What we do:

  • Filter prompts before story generation
  • Use family-friendly AI models
  • Monitor and improve our systems continuously

If you encounter inappropriate content: Please report it to youradventuresp@gmail.com with details so we can improve our filters. We take these reports seriously and use them to enhance our safety systems.

Troubleshooting

The app won't load my stories +

Try these steps:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Close and reopen the app
  3. Sign out and sign back in
  4. Update to the latest app version

If the issue persists, contact youradventuresp@gmail.com

My credits aren't showing after purchase +

1. Wait 1-2 minutes for processing

2. Close and reopen the app

3. Try "Restore Purchases" in Settings

4. If still missing after 5 minutes, contact youradventuresp@gmail.com with your purchase receipt

Story generation failed - did I lose a credit? +

If story generation fails due to a technical error, your credit is automatically refunded. Check your credit balance to confirm.

If you believe a credit was incorrectly charged, contact youradventuresp@gmail.com with details.

The app crashes or freezes +

1. Make sure you're running the latest version

2. Restart your device

3. Reinstall the app (your data is saved to the cloud)

4. If crashes continue, email youradventuresp@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version
